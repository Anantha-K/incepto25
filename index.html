ABOUT INCEPTO

INCEPTO'25 is an event organized by the Department of Civil Engineering, FISAT.
Spanning across two days on 31st January and 1st February 2025; the event will bring out your technical skills, professionalism and expertise.

ABOUT DEPT

The Department of Civil Engineering at FISAT is known for its commitment to academic excellence and professional development. The department actively organizes events like INCEPTO'25 to foster collaboration, innovation, and knowledge-sharing among students, faculty, and industry professionals.

ABOUT FISAT

The Federal Institute of Science and Technology (FISAT) is located at Hormis Nagar, Mookkannoor, Angamaly, in Ernakulam, Kerala, India. FISAT focuses on excellence in education, providing students with opportunities to grow and excel academically and professionally.
