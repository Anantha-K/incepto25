ABOUT INCEPTO

INCEPTO'25 is an event organized by the Civil Engineering Department of FISAT. It is scheduled for January 30th and 31st, and aims to bring students, professionals, and sponsors together for knowledge-sharing and collaboration. INCEPTO'25 also seeks sponsorships to support and enhance the event, offering numerous promotional benefits for sponsors.

ABOUT DEPT

The Department of Civil Engineering at FISAT is known for its commitment to academic excellence and professional development. The department actively organizes events like INCEPTO'25 to foster collaboration, innovation, and knowledge-sharing among students, faculty, and industry professionals.

ABOUT FISAT

The Federal Institute of Science and Technology (FISAT) is located at Hormis Nagar, Mookkannoor, Angamaly, in Ernakulam, Kerala, India (Pin: 683 577). FISAT focuses on excellence in education, providing students with opportunities to grow and excel academically and professionally.
